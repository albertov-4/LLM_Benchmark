These are format examples for the fo-sailing domain.
They are not solutions to the current problem.
Do not copy object names unless they appear in the current problem.

Valid action examples:
(accelerate boat-a)
(go_est boat-a)
(decelerate boat-a)
(save_person boat-a person-a)

Invalid outputs:
(go_east boat-a)
(saved person-a)
(= (x boat-a) 10)
(increase (v boat-a) 1)

Why invalid:
- go_east is not an action in this domain; use go_est exactly
- predicates from the state or goal are not actions
- numeric assignments and effects are not actions

A valid final answer is only a sequence of executable actions, one per line:
(accelerate boat-a)
(go_north_east boat-a)
(decelerate boat-a)
(save_person boat-a person-a)
