These are format examples for the block-grouping domain.
They are not solutions to the current problem.
Do not copy object names unless they appear in the current problem.

Valid action examples:
(move_block_up block-a)
(move_block_right block-a)
(move_block_down block-b)
(move_block_left block-b)

Invalid outputs:
(= (x block-a) 2)
(>= (y block-a) 0)
(increase (x block-a) 1)
(decrease (y block-b) 1)

Why invalid:
- numeric assignments are not actions
- numeric comparisons are not actions
- action effects are not actions

A valid final answer is only a sequence of executable actions, one per line:
(move_block_up block-a)
(move_block_right block-a)
(move_block_right block-a)
