These are format examples for the fo-counters domain.
They are not solutions to the current problem.
Do not copy object names unless they appear in the current problem.

Valid action examples:
(increase_rate counter-a)
(increment counter-a)
(decrement counter-b)
(decrement_rate counter-a)

Invalid outputs:
(= (value counter-a) 5)
(>= (rate_value counter-a) 1)
(increase (value counter-a) 2)
(max_int)

Why invalid:
- numeric assignments are not actions
- numeric comparisons are not actions
- action effects and numeric fluents are not actions

A valid final answer is only a sequence of executable actions, one per line:
(increase_rate counter-a)
(increment counter-a)
(decrement_rate counter-a)
