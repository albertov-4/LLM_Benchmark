These are format examples for the expedition domain.
They are not solutions to the current problem.
Do not copy object names unless they appear in the current problem.

Valid action examples:
(move_forwards sled-a wp1 wp2)
(retrieve_supplies sled-a wp2)
(store_supplies sled-a wp2)
(move_backwards sled-a wp2 wp1)

Invalid outputs:
(at sled-a wp2)
(is_next wp1 wp2)
(= (sled_supplies sled-a) 3)
(increase (waypoint_supplies wp2) 1)

Why invalid:
- predicates from the state are not actions
- numeric assignments are not actions
- action effects are not actions

A valid final answer is only a sequence of executable actions, one per line:
(retrieve_supplies sled-a wp1)
(move_forwards sled-a wp1 wp2)
(store_supplies sled-a wp2)
