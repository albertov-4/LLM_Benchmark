TASK FAMILY: expedition

You are solving a PDDL planning problem with numeric supplies.

The domain models sleds traveling along ordered waypoints. Sleds consume supplies
when moving and may store supplies at, or retrieve supplies from, waypoints.

Use only the action schemas defined in the domain:
- move_forwards
- move_backwards
- store_supplies
- retrieve_supplies

Action interpretation:
- move_forwards moves a sled from waypoint w1 to the next waypoint w2 when
  (is_next w1 w2) holds and the sled has at least 1 supply.
- move_backwards moves a sled from waypoint w1 to the previous waypoint w2 when
  (is_next w2 w1) holds and the sled has at least 1 supply.
- store_supplies transfers 1 supply from a sled to its current waypoint.
- retrieve_supplies transfers 1 supply from the current waypoint to the sled,
  only if waypoint supplies are available and the sled is below capacity.

Planning rules:
- Track each sled location after every move.
- Track sled_supplies, sled_capacity, and waypoint_supplies numerically.
- Respect is_next direction: forwards and backwards use different argument
  relationships.
- Do not retrieve supplies beyond sled capacity.
- Ensure all final location and numeric goals are satisfied.

Output rules:
- Output only executable actions.
- Do not output predicates from the state, numeric assignments, effects, goals,
  or intermediate states.
- Do not output expressions such as (at ...), (is_next ...), (= ...),
  (increase ...), or (decrease ...).
- Each line must be one grounded action using only sled and waypoint names from
  the problem.
