These are format examples for the rover domain.
They are not solutions to the current problem.
Do not copy object names unless they appear in the current problem.

Valid action examples:
(navigate rover-a wp1 wp2)
(sample_soil rover-a store-a wp2)
(drop rover-a store-a)
(calibrate rover-a camera-a objective-a wp2)
(take_image rover-a wp2 objective-a camera-a color)
(communicate_soil_data rover-a lander-a wp2 wp2 wp1)

Invalid outputs:
(in rover-a wp2)
(have_soil_analysis rover-a wp2)
(= (energy rover-a) 20)
(decrease (energy rover-a) 8)

Why invalid:
- predicates from the state are not actions
- achieved facts are not actions
- numeric assignments and effects are not actions

A valid final answer is only a sequence of executable actions, one per line:
(navigate rover-a wp1 wp2)
(sample_soil rover-a store-a wp2)
(communicate_soil_data rover-a lander-a wp2 wp2 wp1)
