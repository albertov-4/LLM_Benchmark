These are format examples for the settlersnumeric domain.
They are not solutions to the current problem.
Do not copy object names unless they appear in the current problem.

Valid action examples:
(build-cabin place-a)
(fell-timber place-a)
(build-sawmill place-a)
(saw-wood place-a)
(build-cart place-a)
(move-laden-cart place-a place-b wood)
(load vehicle-a place-a coal)
(unload vehicle-a place-b coal)

Invalid outputs:
(available wood place-a)
(has-cabin place-a)
(= (available wood place-a) 2)
(increase (labour) 1)
(assign (space-in vehicle-a) 5)

Why invalid:
- predicates from the state or goal are not actions
- numeric assignments are not actions
- action effects are not actions

A valid final answer is only a sequence of executable actions, one per line:
(build-cabin place-a)
(fell-timber place-a)
(build-sawmill place-a)
(saw-wood place-a)
